Baking is a wonderful past time to share with all the family. Whether you're baking alone or with others, the result can be enjoyed by all.
There is nothing worse than finding the perfect recipe and the measurements are in cups, when you use ounces or you don't have some of the ingredients on hand, and you need to make it that immediately.
The aim of this website is to provide a hub of recipes, substitutions and an easy to access converter while doing the recipes. There is also a video on the proper way to measure ingredients at the bottom of the converter page (this is not available in the tablet and mobile versions). See source code for image references.
