Carrot Cake


Converter

From:  

To:  




  • Ingredients

  • For the Cake:
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 4 eggs
  • 450g sugar
  • 125g pecans, chopped
  • 200g carrots, grated
  • 236ml vegetable oil
  • 220g flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon

  • For the Icing:
  • 55g butter
  • 226g cream cheese
  • 456g icing sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees celsius
    2.  
  2. Prepare the cake
    Heat the oven to 200 degrees celsius
    3.  
  3. In a bowl, combine sugar, baking powder, flour, cinnamon and baking soda.
    Add the eggs and oil and mix well.
    4.  
  4. Add the carrots and pecans
    5.  
  5. Pour the mixture into a greased baking tin.
    Bake for 35minutes or until done
    6.  
  6. When done, remove cake from tin and cool.
    7.  
  7. Prepare the icing
    Mix cream cheese and butter until smooth.
    Add sugar and vanilla.
    8.  
  8. Spread on cooled cake
    9.
