Chocolate Brownie


Converter

From:  

To:  




  • Ingredients

  • 185g unsalted butter
  • 185g dark chocolate
  • 85g plain flour
  • 40g cocoa powder
  • 50g white chocolate
  • 50g milk chocolate
  • 3 large eggs
  • 275g golden caster sugar

Method

  1. Heat oven to 160 degrees celsius
    2.  
  2. Place the butter (chunked) and dark chocolate (in small chunks) in a bowl and heat over a saucepan of boiling water (bain marie) until melted. Wait for it to cool.
    3.  
  3. Into a bowl, sieve the cocoa powder and flour.
    Chop the white and dark chocolate into chunks and add them to the bowl
    4.  
  4. Add the eggs and sugar and beat till the mixture is smooth.
    5.  
  5. Pour the mixture into a greased baking tin and place in the oven and cook for 25minutes until firm.
    6.  
  6. When they are done, cut into squares and turn out onto a wire rack to let them cool.
    7.
