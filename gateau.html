Black Forest Gateau


Converter

From:  

To:  




  • Ingredients

  • For the cake:
  • 175g slated butter
  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 300g plain flour
  • 375g golden caster sugar
  • 25g cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 200g buttermilk or natural yogurt
  • To assemble:
  • 425g pitted cherries (canned)
  • 100g cherry jam
  • 4 tablespoons kirsch or cherry juice
  • 500ml double cream
  • 2 tablespoons icing sugar
  • 1 small punnet of fresh cherries

Method

  1. Heat oven to 180 degrees celsius.
    Grease two 3x20cm cake tin
    2.  
  2. Place the butter (chunked) and dark chocolate (in small chunks) in a bowl and heat over a saucepan of boiling water (bain marie) until melted. Wait for it to cool.
    3.  
  3. In a bowl, mix together the flour, sugar, cocoa and bicarbonate of soda.
    4.  
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and buttermilk (or yogurt) together. Add the chocolate mixture to this.
    Add 100ml boiling water and whisk till lump free
    5.  
  5. Divide the mixture between the tins and place in the oven for 25minutes.
    Make sure they are springy and firm.Turn out and cool.
    6.  
  6. Mix together 2 tablespoons of cheery juice from the can and the kirsch. Drizzle over the cakes.
    7.  
  7. Combine the cherries and the jam. Add the 200ml of cream and remainder of the chocolate (melted).
    8.  
  8. Whisk the remaining cream and icing sugar together until soft.
    Spread over two cakes and stack all the cakes together. Spread the choloclate cream mixture ove rhte top cake and pile the fresh cherries on top.
