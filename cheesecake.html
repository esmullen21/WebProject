Cheesecake


Converter

From:  

To:  




  • Ingredients

  • For the Crust:
  • 280g flour, all purpose
  • 340g butter, softened
  • 115g sugar
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 teaspoon orange extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • For the Filling:
  • 5x8oz(226.8g) packges cream cheese
  • 395g sugar
  • 60ml heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 2 large egg yoks
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon lemon extract
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon orange extract

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees celsius
    2.  
  2. Prepare the crust
    In a large bowl, beat flour, butter sugar, egg yolk and leomn extract together
    Shape dough into ball, wrap with cling film and refrigerate for 1 hour.
    3.  
  3. Unwrap the dough and press into the base of a 10inch x 2 1/2inch springform pan
    Bake for 8-10minutes. Cool. Heat oven to 250 degrees celsius
    4.  
  4. Prepare the filling
    Beat cream cheese until smooth. Then beat in the sugar, gream, flour, egg yolks, eggs and extracts.
    Beat until smooth.
    5.  
  5. Pour filling into crust. Bake for 12minutes.
    6.  
  6. Switch temperture to 150 degrees celsius and bake for a further 35 minutes.
    7.  
  7. Remove from oven and cool. When cooled, referigate for 4 hours, till firm.
    8.  
  8. When firm, carefully release the sides of the pan and serve
    9.
